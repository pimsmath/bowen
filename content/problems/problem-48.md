Suppose $ F: C \to \mathbb{R}, C$ the Cantor set, has bounded total variation.
Is there a homeo $g : [0,1] \to [0,1]$ and a diffeo (Lipschitz, maybe)
$f:[0,1] \to \mathbb{R}$ such that \\[ F = f\circ g |C.\\]
