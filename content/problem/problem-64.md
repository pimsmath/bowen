Entropy of group actions. There is no smooth $\mathbb{R}^n $- (or
$\mathbb{Z}^n$-) action with positive entropy when $n >1$. Is this true for
all Lie groups (or lattices) of dimension greater than 1? Try $N$ nilpotent.
